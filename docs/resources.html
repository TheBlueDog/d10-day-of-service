Northern Virginia Intergroup

The Northern Virginia Intergroup (NVI) is a service resource maintained by more than 400 Alcoholic Anonymous groups in our area. Our primary purpose is to help the alcoholic who still suffers. NVI works with the groups in the area to help carry the message.

Virginia's Blue Ridge AA Intergroup

This list covers the 11 Virginia counties of Clarke, Culpeper, Fauquier, Frederick, Loudoun, Madison, Orange, Page, Rappahannock, Shenandoah, Warren, and the City of Winchester.
